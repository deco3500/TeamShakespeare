A unique and interactive way to collect NEWS around you!

Using technology of Force Acceleration and Geolocation to collect the news nearby, by shaking your mobile phone and setting radius within which news has happened.

Learn More

Our Objective  

Team Shakespeare is working on the Mobile App “Shake Shake“, which is aimed to save users’ time through a built-in news search function to dig the news around the user by simply shaking his smartphone. The user will always be updated with the news he wants within the radius using shake function.

Get Started!

Problem Space  

Allodoxaphobia

People feel difficult when making the decision on reading what type of news while news flooding onto the screen.

Laziness

People get used to fast-paced life and are not willing to spend a long time to read a long news article.

Limited Interaction

There are only two ways -- receiving and reading in traditional news reading. People have been getting bored through these two ways gradually.

Passive Exploration

People find it boring to explore the news since all news have already fed to their phones.
